Design Philosophy

Core Concept

Concentric circles represent layered animation systems. The arc symbolizes smooth motion transitions, while flowing lines below indicate animation direction and fluidity.

Technical Precision

Pure SVG with gradient overlays. Minimal line work ensures clarity at any scale—32px favicon to billboard size. No fills, only intentional strokes.

Motion Semantics

The upward arc suggests elevation and flow. Descending motion lines convey animation direction—capturing the essence of frame-by-frame transitions.

Brand Alignment

Purple gradient mirrors MAUI ecosystem. Modern, minimalist aesthetic fits .NET developer tooling. Feels at home in GitHub, NuGet, and Visual Studio.
